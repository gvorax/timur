Bussines trip

Me at Marry Brown

It was an amazing experience to take part in the cooking process of the most famous fried chicken. It wasn’t just regular cooking; I understood that to fry anything in a restaurant as big as Marry Brown, you have to follow strict requirements. For example, brown meat in the chicken, such as thighs and legs, should cook for 8 minutes. However, white meat, such as breast, should cook less—usually for 4-5 minutes. Even though it was a business meeting, not a cooking class, this process helped me better understand the milestones of the business.
